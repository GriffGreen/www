The DAO

A DAO, or Decentralized Autonomous Organization, is a form of company which operates entirely on the blockchain: its governance is transparent, its finances can be audited by anyone, it suffers zero downtime and corruption is impossible.

The DAO is composed of DAO token holders that can review proposals and cast their votes to elect and direct a service provider which will represent them in the physical world, by default Slock.it GmbH. The DAO can replace the service provider at anytime, a process very similar to selecting a new vendor or supplier, which insures that the DAO participants will stay in control of its funds at all times.
